# Shortcuts

Hold `Ctrl` / `Command`:

Move the cursor to any of the four edges, then drag to skew the element.

Move the cursor to a control point, then drag to rotate the element.

Hold `Alt`: center scaling / skewing (`Alt` + `Shift` can be combined).

Hold `Shift`: multi-select / fixed-ratio scaling / fixed-direction movement / rotate around diagonal center.

## Customization

Shortcuts can be customized by listening to [keyboard events](../../../../reference/event/ui/Key.md) and dynamically modifying the [editor configuration](../config/base.md).
