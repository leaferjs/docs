# Sponsor LeaferJS

##

Hi, hello, I’m Chao (Leafer) Wan, the creator of LeaferJS. Thank you for coming to support LeaferJS～

**LeaferJS** is an innovative engine that requires **years** to build and refine (early 2021 - future).

We are not only committed to creating cutting-edge 2D and future 3D engine technologies, but also aim to establish a simple, open, and modern UI drawing standard. This enables developers to quickly build visual productivity tools for the AI era, including web apps, applications, design tools, canvases, games, and animations—driving the next generation of locally-first innovations such as Figma, Miro, Notion, Unity, Adobe, Canva, Webflow, and more, while inspiring further creative technologies and applications.

To promote the creation of more innovative productivity tools, we have open-sourced LeaferJS under the MIT License. **This means you can use LeaferJS for free and apply it in any commercial scenario, truly unlocking the potential of technology**.

## Community

From the moment LeaferJS was open-sourced, it no longer belonged to a single individual, but became a shared community that we all build and maintain together.

When you choose to use LeaferJS, you become a member of this vibrant community and step into this evolving “technical castle.” **Only through the passionate participation, sponsorship, and contributions of every member can this castle continue to grow and improve.** Every contribution you make is a seed that helps you gain richer features and stronger community resources in return.

**Every sponsorship you make is not only recognition of LeaferJS, but also a driving force for technological innovation and the open-source ecosystem.** It helps us focus on development, continuously optimize performance, attract top developers, motivate outstanding contributors, and deliver more powerful and innovative features and services to developers worldwide.

Let’s work together to build a brighter future for LeaferJS!
