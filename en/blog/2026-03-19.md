## Tell Your Story and Let More People See It

Throughout the growth of LeaferJS, we’ve seen many real and inspiring stories:

Some people use it for architectural drawing, some for poster editing, some for animation software, some for AI infinite canvas, and some for container status monitoring at ports.

## ✍️ How to Participate (Very Low Barrier)

You have 2 ways to participate:

### ✅ Option 1: Publish a Full Article (Recommended)

You can publish your story on:

- Juejin / Zhihu / Xiaohongshu, etc.

👉 High-quality content will be officially recommended and featured

### ✅ Option 2: Submit Story Materials (Takes Only Minutes)

If you don’t have time to write a full article, that’s totally fine 👇

You only need to provide:

- 📌 What product you built (one sentence)
- ❗ What problem you were solving (the more real, the better)
- 🔍 Why you chose LeaferJS
- ⚙️ How you solved the problem
- 📈 Final results (performance / experience / outcomes)
- 🖼 One product screenshot (brand can be hidden)

👉 We’ll organize it into a complete story and confirm with you before publishing.
