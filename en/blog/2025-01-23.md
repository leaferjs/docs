# “Blossoming Joy”: From Dream Inspiration to a Dream-Realizing Journey at the Tencent Cloud AI Hackathon

> 2025-01-23 "Blossoming Joy"

嗨，朋友们，小年好！Hi friends, happy Little New Year! I’m Leafer, an open-source creator who loves sharing innovative stories (founder of LeaferJS). Today is the Little New Year in southern China, and I’d like to share a story about “Blossoming Joy”—a journey of drawing inspiration from a dream and turning it into reality.今天是南方小年，我想和大家分享一个关于“心花怒放”的故事，一段从梦境中汲取灵感并在现实中圆梦的旅程。

## Inspiration from a Dream

认识我的朋友可能都听过这样一个有趣的小插曲：有一天，我做了一个奇怪的梦。梦中，我翻阅一本书，最后定格在“心花怒放”四个字上。醒来后，我隐隐觉得这是个天赐的灵感。于是，“心花怒放”成了我与腾讯云共同策划的黑客松比赛的一个主题。

The core idea of this theme is: to let developers use AI programming to create a game filled with positive energy, presenting joy and life goals through interaction.

## Before the Competition: Shanghai Snacks and Night Views

2025 年 1 月 11 日下午，我从北京飞往上海，准备参加腾讯云举办的 AI 开发黑客松比赛。这次活动的地点是在华东理工大学，而我则以评审老师和颁奖嘉宾的身份参与。

As soon as I arrived in Shanghai, I couldn’t wait to head to Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street to try various local delicacies. In the evening, I walked along Nanjing Road to the Bund, enjoying the night view of the Oriental Pearl Tower by the Huangpu River under neon lights. At that moment, even the air felt relaxing.傍晚，我沿着南京路漫步至外滩，在霓虹灯辉映下的黄浦江边欣赏东方明珠的夜景。那一刻，连空气中都弥漫着惬意。

![](/image/blog/20250123/01.jpg)

非常感谢负责订票的腾讯小姐姐，贴心地安排了一家舒适的酒店。这一晚，我怀着期待和愉悦入眠，准备迎接第二天的精彩赛事。

![](/image/blog/20250123/00.jpg)

## The Competition: Surprises and Laughter On Site

On January 12, I set off early. However, I almost got lost because I entered the campus from the East Gate closest to the hotel (the South Gate had clearer directions). Fortunately, I eventually found the venue and avoided an awkward situation.

After arriving, I met Zhang Jingjing, the product lead of Tencent Cloud Cloud Studio—she looked even more beautiful in person than in photos! I also met Ding Ning, the product lead of Tencent Cloud AI Code Assistant, who has been actively promoting our collaboration. Soon after, the event officially began.还有腾讯云 AI 代码助手的产品负责人丁宁，他一直在推动我们之间的合作。随后，活动很快正式开始。

![](/image/blog/20250123/1.jpg)

这次活动邀请了重量级人物，包括华东理工大学副校长王慧锋、腾讯云教育行业总经理杨晖，以及 CSDN 技术合伙人陈玉龙等。他们的欢迎致辞充满了对 AI 技术未来发展的美好展望。

![](/image/blog/20250123/2.jpg)

![](/image/blog/20250123/3.jpg)

Before the competition, Zhang Jingjing, product lead of Tencent Cloud Cloud Studio, explained the rules and demonstrated the Cloud Studio online programming environment and AI Code Assistant. Participants were required to develop creative game applications around the theme “Blossoming Joy” using LeaferJS and Tencent Cloud AI Code Assistant. Tencent Cloud also prepared generous prizes for outstanding teams, such as smart speakers, Bluetooth earphones, custom figurines, and themed backpacks.参赛者们需要围绕“心花怒放”这一主题，利用 LeaferJS 和腾讯云 AI 代码助手，开发出创意十足的游戏应用。腾讯云还为表现优异的团队准备了丰富的奖品，如智能音箱、蓝牙耳机、定制公仔和主题背包等。

After just 1.5 hours, the competition entered the project presentation and judging phase. The judges and I raced against time to score the entries, and finally, the product lead of Tencent Cloud AI Code Assistant announced the results.

![](/image/blog/20250123/6.jpg)

The participants’ creativity and technical skills were truly impressive. Here are a few works that stood out to me:

### **First: The Most Stunning “Blossoming Joy” Visual Effects**

这款作品通过视觉效果征服了大家。This project captivated everyone with its visual effects. The interactive animations were extremely well done—the blooming petals resembled New Year fireworks, smooth and dazzling, earning applause from the audience.

[https://cloudstudio.net/a/24138733074096128/learn](https://cloudstudio.net/a/24138733074096128/learn)

![](/image/blog/20250123/d1.jpg)

### **Second: The “Academic Journey” from Elementary School to University**

这是一对父子合作完成的作品，设计了一段趣味十足的“晋升玩法”。This was a project completed by a father and son, featuring a fun “progression gameplay.” Players needed to complete tasks to advance from elementary school to university (earning certificates). The ending was cleverly designed: the faster you finish, the better the university you enter (top-tier or regular), otherwise you “fail the exam.” It brought continuous laughter to the audience.这个作品逗得全场笑声不断～

[https://cloudstudio.net/a/24079209926508544/learn](https://cloudstudio.net/a/24079209926508544/learn)

![](/image/blog/20250123/p1.jpg)

### **Third: The Subtle Balance Between Goals and Achievements**

这个作品设置了多个子目标，例如外语学习、投资理财和旅行计划等。每完成一个任务，就会点亮一部分“心花怒放”的特效，所有任务完成后还有一个盛大的终极展示。尽管难度较高，但整体创意令人深刻。

[https://cloudstudio.net/a/24137321909460992/learn](https://cloudstudio.net/a/24137321909460992/learn)

![](/image/blog/20250123/d3.jpg)

## New Year Message

值此新年来临之际，我诚挚地邀请开发者们去创造出更多充满正能量、有年味的游戏。As the New Year approaches, I sincerely invite developers to create more games filled with positive energy and festive spirit. Let’s use technology and creativity to bring motivation and joy to people—ignite hope in games and pursue dreams in reality.

“Blossoming Joy” has given me a deeper understanding of a unique meaning—the happiness and sense of achievement when striving toward a goal.

人生何尝不是一场游戏？每个目标，都是一次“心花怒放”的挑战。

May everyone achieve their own “Blossoming Joy” moment in the coming year!
