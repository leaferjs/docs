# Had a Very Strange Dream Last Night

> 2024-11-09 "Blossoming Joy"

Hello everyone, I’m Leafer. Recently, I’ve been struggling while planning a new game development competition with Tencent, hoping to find a theme that resonates with everyone.

### A Strange Dream

This morning, when I woke up, I realized I had a very strange dream. In the dream, I was constantly flipping through books searching for answers, and in the end, four words remained: "Blossoming Joy". It felt like they were telling me that this should be the theme of the competition. I immediately looked up the meaning of "Blossoming Joy" online: a feeling of happiness blooming like flowers in the heart. Isn’t this exactly the state of mind we all long for? At that moment, I had my answer.

### Blossoming Joy

I’d love to know—what is your “Blossoming Joy” wish?
