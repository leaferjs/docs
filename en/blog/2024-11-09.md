# Had a Very Strange Dream Last Night

> 2024-11-09 "Blossoming Joy"

Hello everyone, I’m Leafer. Recently, I’ve been struggling while planning a new game development competition with Tencent, hoping to find a theme that resonates with everyone.

### A Strange Dream

This morning, when I woke up, I realized I had a very strange dream. In the dream, I was constantly flipping through books searching for answers, and in the end, four words remained: "Blossoming Joy". It felt like they were telling me that this should be the theme of the competition. I immediately looked up the meaning of "Blossoming Joy" online: a feeling of happiness blooming like flowers in the heart. Isn’t this exactly the state of mind we all long for? At that moment, I had my answer.我马上网上查了一下"心花怒放"的解释：心里高兴得像花儿盛开一样。这不正是我们每个人都渴望的精神状态吗？我心中顿时有了答案。

### 心花怒放

For an open-source developer who has been创业 for nearly four years and is still in the investment phase, a moment of Blossoming Joy is truly needed.

**或许生活在当前时代潮流下的网友们也和我一样，心里都期待着一次 心花怒放 的时刻。**

I’d love to know—what is your “Blossoming Joy” wish?

Let me share two of mine first:

1. To help LeaferJS’s open-source technology benefit over 10 million developers worldwide as quickly as possible.
2. Quietly making a second wish… haha!

欢迎在评论区分享，祝大家的心愿能像花儿一样盛开，希望我们每个人都能迎来心花怒放的时刻～
