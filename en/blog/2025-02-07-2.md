Found a bug or have suggestions for improvement? Welcome to [submit an issue](https://github.com/leaferjs/leafer-ui/issues) on GitHub. Your contribution helps make LeaferJS better!

## Need Help with Usage?

Encountering unclear issues or missing features during use? We provide multiple support channels and are also recruiting community administrators.

### 1. Ask AI (Coming Soon)

We plan to train and integrate external AI assistants, expected to launch **within the next few months**, which will automatically answer LeaferJS-related questions. Stay tuned!

### 2. Community Support (Recommended)

[Go to Zhihu](https://www.zhihu.com/topic/736459154/unanswered) to create a question and bind the topic [#LeaferJS](https://www.zhihu.com/topic/736459154/unanswered):

::: tip Submission Template
**【leaferjs】xxxxxxxxxxx Title xxxxxxxxxxxxxx**

Environment: PC + Windows 11 + Chrome 131 + leaferjs 2.0.8

<br/>

#LeaferJS (Click the # icon at the bottom of the Zhihu question box to bind the topic)
:::

Community administrators and I will regularly review and respond to questions. You can also share your question link in the [LeaferJS technical discussion group](https://leaferjs.com/#contact). These questions will be indexed by search engines, making it easier for others to find answers and avoid repetitive work.

Consistently providing helpful answers will be considered as an intention to become a LeaferJS community administrator. You can [contact us](https://leaferjs.com/#contact) to join the admin group.

### 3. Official Support

Due to limited resources, we prioritize support for [Gold-tier sponsors and above](../sponsor/index.md#开发赞助) and community administrators (such as priority bug fixes and feature enhancements). As resources grow, we will recruit more professionals and develop systematic LeaferJS tutorials covering everything from beginner to advanced levels.

## Technical Discussion

Welcome to join the [LeaferJS technical discussion group](https://leaferjs.com/#contact) to communicate with other developers, share experiences, and improve the product together.

There are occasional **special events and benefits**, so don’t miss out!

## ⏰ 4-Day Workweek

LeaferJS is trialing a 4-day workweek (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday).

Please take note of working hours when seeking support. Thank you for your understanding and support.
